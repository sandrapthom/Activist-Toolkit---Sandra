Actions to Take
  1. Provide women role models and mentors: Knowing that women are strong and can achieve their dreams is important to show young kids.
  2. Engage girls in STEM experiences: Young girls should know that these STEM fields are open to them. They can build an interest in this area at a young age.
  3. Emphasize the importance of STEM: Girls will be able to find value in their learning.
  4. Create an environment that is not competitive: Women will feel more confident in their skills with less competition.
  5. Provide Encouragement: If women show interest in this field, it is important they they are given support.
